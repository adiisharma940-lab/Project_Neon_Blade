Born American

The Dodge Challenger is a rebellion on wheels — born 1970, resurrected 2008, transformed into a supercharged monster with the SRT Hellcat in 2015. No turbos. No hybrid systems. A 6.2L supercharged HEMI V8 screaming 717 horsepower through the rear wheels. The loudest, fastest, most unhinged muscle car ever sold at a dealership.

Discontinued after 2023 — the last of its kind.
