Heritage // Legacy

Born to Dominate

The Nissan GT-R is not merely a car — it is a philosophy forged in steel, rubber, and relentless pursuit of perfection. Rooted in the legendary Skyline lineage that terrorized 1990s circuits, the R35 generation arrived in 2007 and redefined what a production car could achieve. It embarrassed Ferraris and Porsches at half their price.

The 2013 Black Edition represents the pinnacle of this evolution: factory-tuned, performance-optimized, wearing its intentions on every sculpted surface. Dark chrome accents, blacked-out Rays forged wheels, exclusive interior stitching — this is the sharper edge of an already razor-sharp machine.
